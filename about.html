About

i C. infinity is Luke Nathan Hayes' artist name. It is shorthand for “I see infinity. I choose infinity.” The songs bring together lived experience, island life, science, spirituality, imagination, artificial intelligence and hope.

The music

Albums, songs, lyrics and videos that move from everyday life to cosmic questions.

The ideas

Aura, G.A.J.R.A. Earth, joyful responsible abundance and protopia are different ways of asking how life could become kinder, freer and more alive.

The places

Minjerribah, Brisbane, India and many other places appear through memory, photographs, travel and song.

Strange But True

Strange But True turns unusual ideas into practical local projects, stories and experiments people can see and use.

Why make it?

The art is not measured by search rankings, profit or popularity. If those arrive, useful. If they do not, the music still did what it came here to do.
