Building the grand narrative

What if the future felt worth moving towards?

I See Infinity is an independent creative outlet by Luke Nathan Hayes. It brings songs, systems, place, technology and spiritual self-reflection into one evolving public story.

The invitation is practical: gather digitally and in person, imagine attractive paths towards protopia, and turn joyful responsible abundance from a phrase into lived design.
